Simple Fried Rice Recipe

  1. Prepare day old rice, garlic and egg
  2. Mince 2 cloves of garlic
  3. Whisk the egg until the yolk fully combines with the whites
  4. Put the stove on low heat and add a bit of cooking oil
  5. Pour the minced garlic in and wait for it to change color
  6. Once the garlic change in color, add the egg and mix it constantly
  7. When the eggs are becoming clumped it's time to add the rice
  8. Mix the rice with the eggs thoroughly
  9. Once the egg fully integrated with the rice it's now done
